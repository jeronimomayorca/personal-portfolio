Hello everyone!

My name is Jerónimo, I am a junior developer, student, and passionate about the world of software. I was born in Manizales, Caldas in the mid-2003s. Since I was very young, I have had a strong interest in knowing the reason behind everything.

My grandfather, with whom I used to spend my weekends, taught me how to assemble a PC and install an operating system, and he would often ask me for help to assemble one or two, even when I was just 10 years old!

At home, we had an office computer that served as my school to learn how to download images and music to listen to on my MP3 player. At my 12, I decided to undertake my first project: building a car using a battery, wooden sticks, and a 3-volt motor.

This led me to want to give it instructions to move, thus getting acquainted with Arduino, who was going to be my best friend until I graduated from school. With this platform, I was able to build a robotic arm, which I presented as a science project, and a drone.
With the desire to delve deeper into the strengths of software, I attended a one-year bootcamp at a local startup, where I learned about Python, Angular CLI, TypeScript, software engineering, and design patterns.
Currently, I am studying Systems Engineering at the University of Caldas.
